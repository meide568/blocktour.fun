About BlockTour.fun

Your Daily Puzzle Fix.

Welcome to blocktour.fun! This little corner of the internet was created by a passionate solo developer who believes in the power of a good puzzle to challenge the mind and soothe the soul.

Our mission is simple: to curate a collection of high-quality, addictive, and fun browser games that you can jump into anytime, anywhere. In a world of endless distractions, we believe that everyone deserves a short, satisfying break to reset and recharge.

Every game on this site is hand-picked for its quality gameplay and ability to provide that perfect "puzzle fix." We hope you find your new favorite game here and make us a small part of your daily routine.

Happy puzzling!
