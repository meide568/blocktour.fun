Block Tour

An addictive brain-teasing puzzle game where every move counts.

About The Game

Welcome to Block Tour! This game is a captivating and challenging puzzle adventure inspired by classic "unblock me" style games, often known as Klotski or KFZ puzzles. The objective is simple: guide the main, uniquely marked block to the designated exit. However, the path is obstructed by other blocks that you must strategically slide out of the way.

Challenge your spatial reasoning and planning skills across numerous levels, each more intricate than the last. With its clean visuals and intuitive controls, Block Tour provides a pure puzzle-solving experience that is both relaxing and mentally stimulating.

How to Play

The controls are straightforward, designed for both mouse and touch screens:

  • Click/Tap and Drag: Select any block and drag it horizontally or vertically into an empty space.
  • Objective: Clear a path to move the primary block (the one with the pattern) to the exit area on the right side of the board.
  • Strategy is Key: Think several moves ahead! Sometimes you need to move blocks out of the way to make room for other blocks to move. The fewest moves wins!

Features
